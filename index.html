Professional Bio

I am a dedicated and compassionate Kenya Registered Community Health Nurse (KRCHN) and a graduate of Kenya Medical Training College. My clinical background spans high-pressure environments, including Medical-Surgical units and Maternal & Child Health clinics at Homabay County Teaching and Referral Hospital. I specialize in clinical procedures such as IV medication administration, alongside a strong foundation in KEPI immunization and community health advocacy. I am deeply committed to patient advocacy and providing holistic care that bridges the gap between hospital-based treatment and community-based wellness.
